Black Rock City Mayoral Elections

The 2015 Elections Are Completed

Returns from the 2015 mayoral elections have been tallied and certified by the Black Rock City Elections Commission. The official final count shows incumbent mayor Dr Fox with 4,167 votes, and The Dillman with 4,168 votes, making The Dillman the official new Mayor of Black Rock City.

According to the laws of The State of Nevada, the mayorship of Black Rock City takes effect immediately following the official certification of the voting results, which occurred on Friday, September 4th, at 11:47 PM. Effective immediately, The Dillman is Mayor, and Dr Fox is officially discharged of all mayoral duties.

The concession speech by Dr Fox can be found on the official site for the Dr Fox Campaign.

The Dillman has posted thoughts on his victory on The Party Party website.

As of today the Black Rock City Elections will enter an archived state until the next elections in 2019.
